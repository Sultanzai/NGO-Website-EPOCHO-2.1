Humanitarian Response Program

Our Humanitarian Response Projects at EPOCHO are at the forefront of providing rapid and effective assistance during times of crisis and emergency. With a deep commitment to alleviating suffering and rebuilding lives, we swiftly mobilize resources and teams to deliver vital aid to those affected by natural disasters, conflicts, and other emergencies. Through our Humanitarian Response Projects, we provide emergency relief, shelter, food, medical assistance, and support systems to the most vulnerable populations. With a focus on dignity, compassion, and resilience, we work tirelessly to ensure that no one is left behind in their hour of need. Join us in our Humanitarian Response Projects and become an agent of change, providing hope, comfort, and a lifeline to those facing the greatest challenges. Together, we can make a difference and bring relief to communities in crisis. We do it through grants and fundraisings. EPOCHO donated warm clothing to 1200 street children in 2020 and 2021 , it has established two book clubs for Afghan youth , has supported schools in teaching materials ,teachers’ capacity building trainings , has supported widowed women through creating micro-enterprises, has supported universities and academic institutions’ libraries through book-donation projects under the title of Empowering Minds through Spreading of Reading Culture and currently working on globally fundraisings to start projects focusing education and women’s economically empowerment .

Our Fundraising Projects at EPOCHO are a catalyst for impactful change, enabling us to continue our vital work in education, health, humanitarian response, and women empowerment. Through these projects, we engage with compassionate individuals, organizations, and philanthropic partners who share our vision of creating a better world. By joining hands and contributing to our Fundraising Projects, you become an integral part of our mission, empowering us to reach more communities, deliver critical services, and make a lasting difference in the lives of those in need. Together, we can inspire positive transformation, break barriers, and build a future where every individual has equal opportunities to thrive. Join us in our Fundraising Projects and be a driving force in creating a brighter and more equitable world for all. We are going to achieve the mentioned goals through two ways of fundraising, partnership with Global Giving and Self-financing projects.
